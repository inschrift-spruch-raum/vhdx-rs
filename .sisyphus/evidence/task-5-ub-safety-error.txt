Additional existing tests that reinforce UB safety boundaries:
- test_log_replay_rejects_invalid_leading_trailing_combination (leading=4000+trailing=200>4084)
- log_replay_rejects_invalid_checksum (invalid CRC)
- test_t7_validator_log_rejects_descriptor_count_mismatch (descriptor count mismatch)
- t12_validator_log_rejects_invalid_checksum (validator CRC check)
- t12_validator_log_rejects_guid_mismatch (GUID mismatch)

Conclusion: No Rust-level UB exists. The single unsafe in Log::entry() (log.rs:118-119)
is protected by the while loop invariant: offset + LOG_ENTRY_HEADER_SIZE <= raw.len().
The from_raw_parts(ptr.add(offset), data_len) is safe because data_len = raw.len() - offset,
guaranteeing ptr.add(offset) + data_len == raw.as_ptr() + raw.len(), within the allocation.
